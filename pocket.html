Executive Summary

Pocket Pay is an innovative cashless payment system designed specifically for schools to manage student pocket money and canteen transactions. Our system offers a secure, efficient, and user-friendly platform that benefits students, parents, and school administrators. With Pocket Pay, schools can ensure a smooth, cashless environment that enhances student convenience and promotes financial literacy.
